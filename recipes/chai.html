Chai



Ingredients

  • 5 spoon sugar
  • 3 spoon tea leaves
  • 3 cup milk
  • 1 spoon ginger
  • 1 spoon cinnamon
  • 1 spoon cloves
  • 1 cup water

Directions

  1. Add water
  2. Add tea leaves
  3. Leave it for 1 minute
  4. Add milk
  5. Stir and leave it for 2 minutes
  6. Add sugar
  7. Wait for 2 minutes. And serve it.
